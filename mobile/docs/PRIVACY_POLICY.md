# BurrowMind Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Overview
BurrowMind is a personal wellness tracking application. This policy explains how we handle your data.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Mood entries**: Mood level, factors, notes, timestamps
- **Sleep entries**: Bedtime, wake time, duration, quality
- **Journal entries**: Text content, tags, mood
- **Chat messages**: Conversations with AI companion

### What We Don't Collect
- Location data
- Contact information
- Device identifiers
- Advertising data

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All your personal data is stored **locally on your device** using SQLite encryption. Data is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly sync.

### Authentication
Basic authentication tokens are stored securely using platform-specific secure storage (Keychain/Keystore).

---

## AI Companion

### How It Works
- The AI companion provides supportive responses based on your messages
- Responses are generated using patterns, not a live AI service
- Your conversation history is stored locally

### Limitations
- The AI is NOT a licensed therapist or medical professional
- It does NOT diagnose conditions or prescribe treatments
- For mental health emergencies, please contact a crisis helpline

---

## Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Tap "Delete All My Data"
3. Confirm deletion

This permanently removes all your mood, sleep, journal, and chat data from your device.

### Account Deletion
To delete your account entirely, use the "Delete Account" option in Settings. This removes all data and your account credentials.
